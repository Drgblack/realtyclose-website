RealtyClose is an AI-powered Gmail extension designed specifically for real estate agents. It helps you write professional emails faster, automate follow-ups, and manage client communications more efficiently. Our AI understands real estate context and can draft personalized responses, schedule emails, and organize your inbox automatically.
