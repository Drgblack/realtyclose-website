2025 Real Estate Technology Trends to Watch

From AI assistants to virtual tours, here's what's shaping the future of real estate and how top agents are adapting to stay competitive.

Impact Level: TRANSFORMATIVE

Artificial intelligence is revolutionizing how agents handle email communication. Leading agents are already saving 2-3 hours daily using AI tools that draft personalized responses, schedule follow-ups, and extract key information from client emails.
